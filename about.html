About Ogbako Umu Igbo Dudley

Ogbako Umu Igbo Dudley is a pan-igbo socio-cultural association which encompasses all the igbo people in Dudley, United Kingdom. We aim among other things, to promote unity and bring together NDI IGBO(Igbo People) in United Kingdom and to encourage and promote individual and group resourcefulness and creativity among ndi igbo in the diaspora


Our key Objective is to contribute and facilitate the igbo development, while promoting, protecting and advancing igbo culture and education in the diaspora and igbo land.


We defend and promote igbo culture and interest and encourage all those yet to identify with us, to come on board so we can work together for the betterment of Ndi Igbo.
